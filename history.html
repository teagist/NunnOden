Nunn-Oden Lodge No. 398

Our History

A Legacy of Brotherhood, Service, and Tradition

Introduction

Since its founding, Nunn-Oden Lodge No. 398 has stood as a beacon of Freemasonry in Hartselle, Alabama. Through generations of faithful service, the lodge has upheld the timeless principles of Brotherly Love, Relief, and Truth.

Key Milestones

Chartering of the Lodge

Nunn-Oden Lodge No. 398 was officially chartered, marking the beginning of a long tradition of Masonic work in the Hartselle community.

Early Growth and Community Presence

In its early years, the lodge quickly became a central part of the community, drawing members committed to moral improvement and service.

Construction of the Lodge Building

The lodge established its physical home, creating a lasting space for fellowship, ritual, and community engagement.

Decades of Service

Through wars, economic challenges, and changing times, the lodge continued its work, supporting its members and the broader community.

Modern Day

Today, Nunn-Oden Lodge remains active and vibrant, continuing its mission while embracing the future.

A Living Tradition

The story of Nunn-Oden Lodge is not simply one of dates and events, but of men committed to making themselves better and serving others.

Each generation has added its own chapter, preserving the traditions of the past while shaping the future of the lodge.

Be Part of the Next Chapter

The history of Nunn-Oden Lodge continues to be written today. We invite good men to learn more and take part in this enduring tradition.
