Our History

Nunn-Oden Lodge has faithfully served the brethren of Hartselle and surrounding communities for generations.

Chartered in the early 20th century, Nunn-Oden Lodge No. 398 has stood as a pillar of Freemasonry in Hartselle. Through decades of growth, fellowship, and service, the lodge has remained committed to the principles of Brotherly Love, Relief, and Truth.
